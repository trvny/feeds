Kanarek puts your feeds and your streams on your home screen.

News:

* A home-screen widget that cycles through your RSS/Atom feeds, with headlines, images and a tap-through to the article.
* Add feeds by URL, or point Kanarek at a site and let it discover the feed for you.
* Import and export your subscriptions as OPML via the system file picker — no storage permission needed.
* Feeds are fetched efficiently with conditional requests, and the widget keeps the last good items instead of blanking when a fetch fails.

Radio & IPTV:

* A built-in player for internet radio and IPTV streams, with a second home-screen widget for transport controls.
* Background playback with lock-screen and notification controls.
* Load stations from M3U playlists via the file picker.

Privacy:

* No account, no ads, no tracking.
* Works fully on-device. An optional backend proxy is available, but it is free software (in the same repository) that you can self-host, and Kanarek runs without it.
